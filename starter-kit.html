Codabench

Getting started

Follow these steps to register, access the challenge data, and submit your model.

Step 1 - Register

  1. Read the terms and conditions.
  2. Create accounts on Codabench and AMPLIFAI on Hugging Face.
  3. Register for the challenge by completing the registration form.

Step 2 - Access data

Download the training and validation data from Hugging Face. Approval will be communicated by email to your primary team contact.

Step 3 - Submit your model

For submission instructions, refer to the submission instruction repository on GitHub.

Leaderboard

View rankings on the Results tab on Codabench. Each team may submit up to 5 entries.
